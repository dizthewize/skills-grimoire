Auto-merge mode (auto=true or set in references/conventions.md)
When auto=true is set (default when called from /gh ship):
✦ gh-pr polls PR status every 60s (up to 30 min)
✦ When conditions met (1 approval + CI green) → merges automatically
✦ Reports: "PR #42 auto-merged — [commit hash]"
✦ If conditions not met after 30 min → reports status + exits without merging
gh-issue integration (when issue= is passed)
