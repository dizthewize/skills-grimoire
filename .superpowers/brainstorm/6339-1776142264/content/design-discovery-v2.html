Design Section 2 — site-discovery (revised)

Questions branch by site type. Service-based businesses get a targeted intake flow.

Phase 1 — universal (all site types)
? What is the business name?
? What type of site is this?
→ Service-based business / SaaS / E-commerce / Blog / Content / Portfolio / Landing page
? What city or region does the business serve? (used for local SEO + branding)
Phase 2A — service-based business branch
? What trade or industry?
→ Flooring / Roofing / Remodeling / Landscaping / HVAC / Plumbing / Auto / Mobile Mechanic / Cleaning / Other (describe)
? What is the primary contact phone number? (appears in 3+ placements on site)
? What services do you offer? (list them — becomes the services section)
? What cities/areas do you serve? (service area section + local SEO)
? What is your primary lead capture offer?
→ Free estimate / Free consultation / Free inspection / Discount on first job / Custom (describe)
? Any key trust signals to highlight?
→ Years in business, # customers served, licensed/insured, certifications, warranties
? Do you have brand colors? (or let the skill pick industry-appropriate colors)
? What copy tone fits this business?
→ Bold & direct / Friendly & local / Premium & refined / Technical & expert
? Any competitor or reference sites you admire? (optional)
Phase 2B — SaaS branch (different questions)
Target persona, pricing model, key features, trial/freemium offer, integrations, tech stack preference…
Phase 2C — E-commerce branch (different questions)
Product catalog size, payment processor, shipping logistics, Supabase vs Shopify, collections structure…
Phase 3 — tech decisions (all types)
? Frontend stack? (recommended based on site type: Next.js for SaaS/e-comm, Astro for service/blog)
? Does this site need user accounts / authentication?
→ No / Yes — Supabase Auth / Yes — other (describe)
? Does it need a database or backend?
→ No / Yes — Supabase (preferred, credentials requested now) / Yes — other
? Deployment platform?
→ Netlify via GitHub (default) / Vercel (credentials requested)
Output
→ writes docs/product-marketing-context.md
→ summarizes back to user: "Here's what I captured — does this look right?"
→ only proceeds once confirmed
